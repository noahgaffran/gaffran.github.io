Schwenger 1
This rocket flew in 2019 after my first year. It reached 23,500 ft and placed 6th in class at Spaceport America. I worked on the nosecone and fins, as well as the flight simulations and apogee prediction.
Now For Plan A
This rocket was born on my laptop. I designed the overall concept layout, as well as the aerodynamic components. It flew in 2021 at Spaceport America, reaching 25,500 ft and Mach 1.5. During construction I was responsible for the couplers, fins, nosecone, as well as the finishing and painting of the rocket.
2022-2023
This upcoming academic year will see QRET's first rocket that will fly higher than 30,000 ft and it will be flown at Spaceport America and Launch Canada. A second rocket may also be built. The exact goal and performance is not yet public but I can say it will be very very fast.
