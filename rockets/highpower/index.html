The project
The goal of this project is to achieve my level 1 (and 2) high power rocketry certification from the Canadian Association of Rocketry. The rocket is scratch built. It is a 3" airframe with a 29mm motor mount. It will be test flown on a CTI G57 motor before being flown in the certification attempt on an H motor.
Construction
The fins are 1/4" plywood attached through the wall and affixed with Fibreglast epoxy thickened with thixotropic silica. The shock cord is tubular kevlar, and the parachute 24" nylon.
